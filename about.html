What is Hello Web Currriculum Workshop like ?

Overview:

An event hosted by Mozilla Foundation in India with people from different sub communities based on gender diversity, activism and leadership from within the community and beyond. This event will mainly focus on Curriculum Design with the intent of providing Mozilla Community in the Indian subcontinent with better tools and kits to teach the web undertaking detailed planning, structure and workflow of the contribution pathways.


Goals:

  • To develop viable teaching tools and kits for the community to better teach the web.
  • Develop a strategy, direction and plan for the future of the community.
  • Increase the leadership and mobilization skills of the attendees.
  • To introduce the people from within the community and beyond to curriculum design and its importance.


Schedule

  • Intro to Mozilla Community - Structure/Domains (10:11 AM - 10:20 AM)
  • Session 1: (10:30 AM - 11:30 AM)

Activity- Icebreakers.
Domain-wise split-up.

Curriculum Design Workshop - Attendees build new structure to teaching tools/activities on different domains.  
Hosting Curriculum Design Sprints/Hackathons under Clubs/local communities.
Discuss projects/campaigns that communities have been working on (region wise)
  • Tea Break (03:00 PM - 03:30 PM)
  • Session 3: (03:30 PM - 05:00 PM)
